Description

I have some memories of saying goodbye that I don't want to forget, so I turned them into 11 minigames and backed them up in this collection.

Features

  • "Some Goodbyes We Made" is a minigame collection about saying goodbye. It consists of 11 short minigames, each preserving a precious farewell memory.
  • In this game, players will experience many different farewell stories, including moving houses, graduation ceremonies, quarrels with friends, and more. You'll see how the protagonist preserves the sadness and profundity of their goodbyes through these minigames, and feel the unique complexity, heartache, or beauty of each one.
  • The minigames in the collection include visual novels, platformers, and even character creators. Perhaps they can help you revisit the goodbyes of your own life.
